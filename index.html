Audio Profanity Filter:
Swearing and curse words have the potential to offend — crossing a cultural line into taboo territory. Audio Profanity Filter is a software, that scans audio and video files to filter out any bad words. This tool censors swear words to prevent exposing them to innocent children or any audience.
Foreign Object Debris Detection at Airport
Foreign Object Debris (FOD) at airports includes any object found in an inappropriate location that, as a result of being in that location, can damage equipment or injure personnel or cause flight delays and cancellations. We have developed a solution that detects and reports the presence of any FOD at runways to airport staff in real-time.
